SUBTLETY
IS KEY

The goal of fill-flash is to make the end result look natural. Too much additional illumination can give your image an artificial look, while too little gives the impression that nothing was augmented. Ideally, the appearance of flash should go undetected. The contrast between the light and dark areas should be lessened, yet not appear evident.
